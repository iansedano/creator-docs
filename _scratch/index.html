Content¶

  • stay DRM-free! We don't want to plagiarize.
    • Images: Only use images you made yourself or that are in the public domain. Creative Commons requires attribution, so post a link.
    • External Learning Resources: Link to great external resources you know of. Our main goal is for students to learn the topics. We want external resources to be clearly labeled and an addition to our course content, not a replacement
